Research Achievements

VMD and Wavelet Transform Hybrid Filtering Algorithm

During my project development, I discovered that PPG signals are susceptible to motion artifacts and environmental light interference, resulting in significant high-frequency noise. To enhance data reliability, I researched and implemented a hybrid filtering algorithm combining VMD (Variational Mode Decomposition) and wavelet transform.

This innovative approach effectively separates different frequency components, significantly improving signal reliability without increasing hardware costs. The algorithm's adaptive nature and precise modal separation capabilities provide a substantial leap in monitoring performance and data reliability.

Technical Innovation

Combined VMD's adaptive decomposition with wavelet transform's multi-resolution analysis for superior noise reduction in biomedical signals.

Performance Improvement

Achieved significant enhancement in signal-to-noise ratio while maintaining computational efficiency suitable for embedded systems.

Hardware Compatibility

Designed to operate efficiently on resource-constrained embedded platforms without requiring additional hardware components.

Practical Application

Successfully applied in biomedical monitoring devices, demonstrating real-world effectiveness in challenging signal environments.
